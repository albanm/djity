Welcome to DjityPortal's documentation!
=======================================

DjityPortal is the software behind http://djity.net

It sort of is a cms, or a blog provider or a social network or a web
application framework, etc. depending on your point of view. Anyway it's
designed to help you build and publish rich Web apps for communities of users. It is built upon a
good deal of great open source technologies.
